About us

Our services

Our expertise includes, but is not limited to, the following services:

Environment Clearances

All developmental activities are required to confirm to regulatory requirements to protect the environment and human health and safety.

Environment Impact Assessment

At Ireme, by doing these detailed studies on EIA, we can help our clients with all the technical details and other pertinent information on the proposed project.

Environment Audit

Our Environmental Audit procedure encompasses Pre-Audit Activities, Activities on Site and Post-Audit Activities which provide objective evaluations of how well the operation management systems are performing.

Post EC Monitoring

Ireme with its expertise team assist you and monitor your half-yearly compliance as per EC Conditions and submit its report to the Ministry.

Liasoning

Our liasoning officers and team of technocrats work hand in hand with Government Authorities to get all the necessary consents and approvals before starting up an industry

Risk Assessments & Reduction

Ireme’s Risk Management department take a thorough and unbiased look at what your risks are, and evaluate what can be done to eliminate the risks.
